Ancient Restorative Practice

Yoga Nidra

A guided state of conscious deep rest. 20 minutes of Yoga Nidra is equivalent to ~2 hours of restorative sleep. It calms cortisol, deepens delta-wave recovery, and restructures your relationship with rest.

—
I
Physical Settling (2 min)
Lie flat, arms relaxed away from the body. Close eyes. Release every last ounce of effort.
II
Sankalpa — Intention (1 min)
Whisper mentally: "I sleep deeply and wake fully restored." Feel it as already true in the body.
III
Body Rotation (8 min)
Move awareness rapidly through each body part — thumb, palm, wrist, elbow — without any movement.
IV
Breath Counting (5 min)
Count each breath from 27 down to 1. If you lose count, simply begin again — without judgement.
V
Pairs of Opposites (4 min)
Feel heaviness / lightness, warmth / cold. Experience both in the body fully and completely.
VI
Visualisation (5 min)
Let rapid images rise — a flame, a lotus, a moonlit lake, a starfield. Watch them appear and dissolve.
